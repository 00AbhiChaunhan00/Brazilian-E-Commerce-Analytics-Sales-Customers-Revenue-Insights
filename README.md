## 💡 Key Insights

- 💳 Credit Card dominates customer payments.
- 📈 A few sellers generate a major share of revenue.
- 🛍 Low-priced products drive purchase volume.
- 🌎 Revenue is concentrated in a few states.
- 👥 A small percentage of customers contribute most of the revenue.
